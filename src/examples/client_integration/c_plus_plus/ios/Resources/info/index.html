About this App

The BigWorld Server supports clients running on Windows, PS3, Xbox 360, HTTP and iOS. This app connects to a server that also allows PC clients to log in, and it demonstrates the basic functions of entity movement and communication. The application builds on our existing integration of BigWorld Server with PS3, Xbox360, and PC platform clients.

How to use this App

Your Avatar is the icon initially in the centre of the screen. Tap with one finger to move the Avatar. Use two fingers to zoom in and out, and drag the view around. Tap another icon (these are other players) to get info on them. Press the compass icon to reset the position and rotation of the camera. This will also make the camera follow the player again.
